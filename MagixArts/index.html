About-Us


"Imagination is the beginning of creation. You imagine what you desire, you describe what you think, and at last you implement it into a reality !"


MagixArt is an online editing tool for creating digital artworks, with a goal to encourage users throughout the world to design something of their own and display it wherever they want. Inspite of having a complete application, we provide the users with all the necessary editing tools right on our website. The vibrant colors along with the templates play along well enough to keep the users busy and motivated for more ideologies.


Relative Question


Why does the template look different while printing/saving it as a PDF?


After clicking on save as PDF, a pop-up will appear. The first option is a drop-down box called Destination. Here you have to select "Save as PDF". After this, there's another option called More Settings. Click on it and you'll be seeing at the Options at the end. Here you have to "Uncheck" for Headers and Footers, while "Check" for Background graphics.


Why can't I directly start editing after clicking on "Let's start" ?

You need to login first before editing onto any templates and download your respective PDFs. This is essential for our website as it helps us in sending you our future updates as well as keep you in contact with us.
